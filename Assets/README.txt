
--All Sliders Are Located Under Webcam--
[EFFECTS]
Invert - Inverts colors of webcam using the slider
Brightness - Changes the brightness of the webcam using the slider
Speed - Changes intensity of effects over time based on slider
Grain - Adds noise over the webcam with differing opacity based on the slider
Contrast - Changes contrast of the webcam based on the slider
Shift - Shift the image over the value of the slider that is Red Green and Blue
